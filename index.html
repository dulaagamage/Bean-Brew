About Us

At Bean & Brew in Kotte, Sri Lanka, we’re all about great coffee and warm conversations. Our cozy café is the perfect spot to unwind, connect, and enjoy expertly brewed blends made from the finest beans. Whether you’re grabbing a quick espresso or savoring a slow sip, every cup is crafted with care. Step in, relax, and let the aroma of fresh coffee make you feel at home.
